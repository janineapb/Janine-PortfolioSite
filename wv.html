WeVerse 2.0

This is a redesign of the WeVerse iOS application, a fan community platform that hosts multimedia content and connect fans with BigHit artists. It is a place where fans can share their stories to their idols and also to other fans.

I often use the WeVerse app to see updates from my favorite artist and I felt the current experience in using the app could be enhanced with a more refined user experience.

My Role:

User Research, Information Architecture, Sketching, Wireframing, UI, Prototyping


The Challenge:

The challenge was to redesign the current WeVerse app to allow users feel more confident and at ease with interacting with their favorite artists and other users within the app.

Goal:

Redesign WeVerse iOS app to increase user engagement and enhance the current experience

My Process:


1. Emphatize:

User Research
I wanted to learn more about what people think about the current application aside from my own thoughts with using it. I conducted interviews from my fellow mutual fans in person and through twitter messages. This allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of the fans thoughts and behaviours with interacting with their favorite artists.

My main takeaways from the interviews are:
  • The navigation can be confusing to new users as there’s too much unorganized content. People more likely to use an app with an easy and organized navigation
  • People likes it when they use features they’re already familiar with such as fast and easy to use interfaces
  • Most fans use the app only when there's updates from artist because of the confusing outlook of the app.
User Journey
From my findings, I constructed the User Journey of Joana, to help me understand how can I help our users to achieve their goals using certain features of the app.

2. Define:

Brainstorming

I started to brainstorm ideas and created POV and How Might We Statements from the Insights and Empathy Map.

How might we help Joana navigate easily in the app?
  • Simplify navigation
  • Group similar categories together
How might we redesign the app to be more intuitive to encourage users to engage more on the app?
  • Select content user wants to see first
  • Lock user comment on top
  • Add filters and icons that allow easier access
  • Combine content that can be grouped together
Site Map / Information Architecture
I mapped out features from the current app and changed it to a more organized content that users can be satisfied with. This allows me to have a clear picture of of the features and interactions the users really need.

5. Test

Impact
I tested the WeVerse app redesign to a fellow BTS army and fan who uses the WeVerse App. She tested the redesigned app herself and was really happy of the changes I did with it. She said it was much more organized and easy to use than the current one and that my ideas would be a great improvement to the current app. If she were to choose, she would more likely use the redesigned one than the current one. She also suggested a few ideas that i can use on my next steps.
Reflection
I often use the WeVerse app and it always bugs me how problematic it is to use sometimes. Knowing that the users are satisfied on an improved feature I redesigned makes me uplifted and energized to also improve other products that needs improvement.
Next Steps
Usability testing gave me a few ideas to improve the app and use it on Ideation version 2 such as:
  • Sort comment by popularity or new comments - this feature can help users easily identify what are the comments that captured the attention of many and it can be informative to other users
  • Filter of the popular posts in the army feed - this feature can also be added to the discover bottom navigation
